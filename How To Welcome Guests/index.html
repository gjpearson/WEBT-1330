Who Do We Train?

Let us work with your church staff to help train everyone in your ministry team. There are areas of assistance that you may not even be aware exist.

We'll be happy to come work with your Greeters, Parking Lot Attendants, Ushers, Coffee Shop Volunteers or First Impression Team. We work with anyone that has an impact on greeting your guests."

Have Questions?

Let us resource you for all things First Impressions. Whether you are in need of gift bags, parking lot and building signage or information cards, if we don't have it, we'll find it for you.

We can also get you in touch with other churches in your similar demographic and background to see what works for them. It's a great opportunity to walk through establishing a plan with someone who's walked the same path.
