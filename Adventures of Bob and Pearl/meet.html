Adventures of Bob and Pearl

Let Us Introduce You To Pearl

Pearl is a 2024 Honda Ridgeline that we purchased from Scott Cass at East TN Auto Outlet in Etowah, TN. It’s no secret in our home that Glenda LOVES a nice truck and she only wanted the Obsidian Blue Pearl color... hence our name choice of “Pearl”.

Pearl is a comfortable ride with plenty of leg room in the back. But our favorite part is the tailgate drops down or swings out. Easy access. There’s an additional bonus of a deep covered trunk at the back of the bed. Lots of travel storage.
